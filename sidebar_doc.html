Instructions for Using This Sheet

  • Do not make changes to highlighted columns. Changes made to highlighted columns will not populate the master spreadsheet and will be lost.
  • Changes made to unhighlighted columns will ONLY populate the data hub and be visible to others when you update your work.
  • To update your work, click on Serials Data and then Update Data Hub. The first time you do this, the app will ask for permissions that will only affect this spreadsheet; use your gwu e-mail address and grant the permissions.
  • If you leave the sheet open for a long time, you will need to refresh the page in order to see any changes that may have been made since you opened it.
  • To refresh the page, click on Serials Data then Get Latest Data
  • While you are free to add columns to the spreadsheet, do not delete columns. Also, keep in mind that anyone in the same role (finance, negotiations, etc.) accessing the spreadsheet will be able to see the columns you add.
  • Use Data --> Filter views... to hide unwanted rows.
  • Sorting the data is permitted as it does not affect the data hub or the ability of the sheet to receive new updates.
  • Do not delete rows. If you think rows are present in error, please contact the data hub administrators, Dolsy Smith (dsmith@gwu.edu) or Joyce Whitmore (whitmore@gwu.edu).
